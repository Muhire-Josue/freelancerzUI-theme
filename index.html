About Us

Freelancerz is a web application that help clients to find talented Software Developers

Finding the right people to work on your project is a tough thing to do, Freelancerz will help you get the right people for your project so that you won't have to worry about their credibility.

As a freelance Software Developer, it's not always easy to find reliable clients to work for, it's also not easy to find work or opportunities or simply getting your name out there so that people can send some tasks your way. Freelancerz is not only a tool that will help you start a network and build your skills, but it'll also expose you to all sorts of opportunities.

  • Got an idea for an app? Find talented Software Developers here to bring it to life.
  • Have some free time to make extra money? Find some projects here.
  • We are here to server both the Software Developers and clients equally.

Services

Freelancerz services are well defined and can be categorized in three sections:

Find Developers

Clients can find Software Developers to work on their project and get paid for it. Clients can hire many Developers.

Find Projects

Based on their skills, Software Developers can find different projects to work on and make some money.

Build Reputation

Software Developers can build a Reputation by being rated by their former clients based on performance.

Why choose us?

Because we are the best in the business.

We vet our Software Developers.

Not all Software Developers how apply on our application gets in, we have a thorough vetting system to make sure only the best get through

Report abuses

In case either the client or the Software Developer does something unprofessional or illegal, they can submit a complement through the application and our teams will be following up on that claim as soon as possible.

Get exposed to more opportunities

As a software Developer is cumulating more and more positive ratings they get more exposed to some more projects and earn more money.

Build your Portfolio here

They say "we all have the same talents but we don't all necessarily have access to the same opportunities."

Our sol mission here is to help all Software Developers to get access to opportunities to level up they skills and earn some money along they way.

We give you the platform to build a strong Portfolio by working projects that matters and bring value to your Clients. Accumulate positive ratings and build a robust Reputation all over.

No Need to spend endless hours on calls.

You don't have to spend that much time on the phone trying to find someone who can get the job done. You can hire Software Developers just by a few clicks here and there.

Finding the right people for your beloved project can take time and risk taking, people can make promises that they can't keep. Freelancerz help you get just the right people and do the rest of the work for you.
